# Tableau_HR_Dashboard
Designed and developed an interactive Tableau HR Dashboard to provide comprehensive insights into workforce performance, employee demographics, and HR operations. The dashboard integrated data from multiple HR systems to support strategic workforce planning and data-driven human resource decisions.
# Key HR Metrics & Visualizations:

Tracked core HR KPIs such as total headcount, hiring rate, attrition rate, retention rate, average tenure, and vacancy rate.

Analyzed employee demographics including age, gender, department, job role, location, and experience level to support diversity and workforce distribution analysis.

Visualized recruitment and hiring metrics, such as time-to-hire, cost-per-hire, offer acceptance rate, and source of hire effectiveness.

Monitored employee performance and engagement indicators, including performance ratings, promotions, training completion, and skill development.

# Operational & Analytical Capabilities:

Implemented trend analysis to track workforce growth, attrition, and hiring patterns over time.

Enabled interactive filters and drill-downs by department, location, job level, and employment type for detailed analysis.

Used calculated fields, parameters, and LOD expressions to derive advanced HR metrics and comparisons.

Highlighted high-attrition departments and roles, enabling proactive retention strategies.

# Business Impact:

Improved HR leadership visibility into workforce trends through self-service dashboards, reducing dependency on manual reports.

Supported strategic workforce planning by identifying talent gaps, attrition risks, and future hiring needs.

Enhanced decision-making by presenting HR data in clear, actionable, and executive-friendly visualizations.

# Tools & Techniques Used:

Tableau Desktop / Tableau Server

Data blending and joins from HRIS systems

Calculated fields and parameters

Interactive dashboards with actions and drill-downs

Data quality checks and performance optimization
